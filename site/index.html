Investment Philosophy

Diversification is key.

Rigorous due diligence and evaluation processes are used for each investment opportunity. Understanding and managing the downside risk is essential — capital preservation comes before capital growth.

Diversification

Across asset classes, geographies, managers and time horizons.

Absolute returns

Positive returns targeted across cycles — not benchmark-hugging.

Hedged exposures

Long and short positioning to temper drawdowns and isolate conviction.

Downside discipline

Position sizing governed by loss tolerance, not upside dreams.
